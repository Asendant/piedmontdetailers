# Review Generation Workflow for Post-Launch

## Automated Review Request System

### Email Template for Review Requests

Send this email 24-48 hours after service completion:

**Subject:** How was your Piedmont Detailers experience?

**Body:**
Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for choosing Piedmont Detailers! We hope you're happy with your [Service Package] service.

Would you mind taking a moment to share your experience? Your feedback helps us improve and helps other customers in the Piedmont Triad make informed decisions.

[Review Link - Google Business Profile]

[Review Link - Facebook]

We truly appreciate your business!

Best regards,
Piedmont Detailers Team

### Review Request Timing

- **Best Time:** 24-48 hours after service completion
- **Follow-up:** If no response after 7 days, send a gentle reminder
- **Method:** Email or SMS (with customer permission)

### Review Platforms to Focus On

1. **Google Business Profile** (Highest Priority)
   - Most visible in local search results
   - Directly impacts local SEO rankings

2. **Facebook Reviews**
   - Social proof and visibility
   - Easy for customers to leave reviews

3. **Yelp** (Optional)
   - Less critical but still valuable
   - Good for additional social proof

### Incentives (Optional)

- Small discount on next service (5-10%)
- Entry into monthly drawing
- Free add-on service

### Review Response Strategy

Always respond to reviews (positive and negative):
- **Positive reviews:** Thank the customer, mention specific details
- **Negative reviews:** Apologize, offer to make it right, take conversation offline

### Tracking Reviews

- Monitor review count weekly
- Track average rating
- Respond to all reviews within 48 hours
- Address negative reviews immediately
